Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I get a health check-up?
The frequency of health check-ups depends on various factors such as age, gender, and medical history. Generally, it's recommended to have a comprehensive health check-up annually. However, some individuals may need more frequent check-ups based on their specific health conditions or risk factors.
What should I bring to my laboratory test appointment?
For your laboratory test appointment, please bring:
  • A valid form of identification
  • Your insurance card (if applicable)
  • Any relevant medical records or doctor's orders
  • A list of current medications you're taking
  • Fasting information (if required for your specific test)
How long does it take to get laboratory test results?
The turnaround time for laboratory test results varies depending on the type of test. Some routine tests may provide results within 24-48 hours, while more complex tests might take several days to a week. Our team will inform you about the expected timeframe for your specific test results during your appointment.
